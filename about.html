About Us

Bora Foundation was established in 2018 by Bora Johnson. It is a non-profit organisation dedicated to supporting orphaned children. The Foundation embodies Bora Johnson's visionary commitment to uplifting the most vulnerable members of society, leaving a legacy of hope and empowerment.

Vision

Bora Foundation’s vision is to create a place where orphaned children are not just survivors, but thrivers — where they are given the love, care, and opportunities to unlock their potential and become beacons of hope in the community.

Mission

The foundation will continue to prioritise the well-being of every child through education, psychological support, and healthcare, in order to encourage and build their futures.

The Impact of Your Donations

Bora Foundation is dedicated to creating brighter futures for orphaned children by providing safe shelter, nutritious food, access to education, and psychological support. Guided by our mission to nurture and empower vulnerable young lives, we strive to make a meaningful difference in their lives.
